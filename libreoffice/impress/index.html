    LibreOffice Impress Full Practical Training Guide

    TRAINING SCENARIO (REAL-WORLD)

    Scenario Title:

    Faculty Orientation Program Presentation

    Situation:

    You are an Academic Administrative Officer in a university.

    The Dean has asked you to prepare a presentation for a New Academic Staff Orientation Program.

    The presentation will be delivered by the Dean, so it must:

    • Use a professional template
    • Have smooth slide transitions
    • Include simple animations (not distracting)
    • Contain speaker notes for the presenter
    • Be clear, clean, and easy to understand

    EXPECTED FINAL OUTPUT

    A 10-slide professional presentation including:

    • Official template
    • Animated bullet points
    • Slide transitions
    • Speaker notes for each slide

    FULL STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

    STEP 1: Open LibreOffice Impress

    1. Click Start Menu
    2. Open LibreOffice
    3. Click Impress

    1. Select Blank Presentation
    2. Click Create

    STEP 2: Apply a Template (IMPORTANT)

    Use Built-in Template

    1. Go to File New Templates
    2. Select Presentations

    3. Choose a simple professional template (e.g. Blue Curve)

    4. Click Open

    This applies consistent design to all slides

    STEP 3: Create Title Slide (Slide 1)

    Title:

    New Academic Staff Orientation

    insert "Text Box"

    Slide > Layout > Title and Content

    Subtitle:

    Faculty of Science
University of XYZ
2026

    Keep centered Large readable font

    STEP 4: Agenda Slide (Slide 2)

    Add New Slide

    Slide > New Slide

    add Title and Content

    Slide > Layout > Title and Content 

    Program Agenda

    Bullet Points:

    • Welcome and introduction
    • Faculty structure
    • Academic responsibilities
    • Support services
    • Questions & discussion

    STEP 5: Add Speaker Notes (Slide 2)

    Steps:

    1. Click View Notes Pane

    1. In the notes area, type:
    Briefly explain the purpose of the orientation and introduce the main topics.

    Speaker notes are only visible to the presenter

    1. back to Normal Slide Click View Normal

    STEP 6: Faculty Overview Slide (Slide 3)

    Add New Slide

    Slide > New Slide

    add Title and Content

    Slide > Layout > Title and Content

    Content:

    • Departments in the faculty
    • Number of academic staff
    • Student population
    • Research focus areas

    STEP 7: Add Animations (Slide 3)

    Animate Bullet Points (Content Only)

    1. Click inside the content area (bullet text box) (Do NOT select the slide background or title)

    2. Select the bullet list text

    3. Go to View Animation

    (The Animation panel will open on the right side)

    1. Click Add

    1. In the animation settings, choose:

    2. Animation: Appear

    3. Start: On click

    4. Duration: Medium

    5. Click OK

    6. In the Animation panel, ensure:

    7. Animate as series

    8. By paragraph level

    Bullet points will now appear one by one when clicking during the presentation

    STEP 8: Academic Responsibilities Slide (Slide 4)

    Add New Slide

    Slide > New Slide

    Title

    "Academic Responsibilities Slide"

    Content:

    • Teaching responsibilities
    • Research activities
    • Student supervision
    • Administrative duties

    STEP 9: Add Animation to Title (Optional)

    1. Select slide title
    2. View Animation

    3. Choose:

    4. Animation: Fade In

    5. Start: With previous

    Keep animations minimal and professional

    STEP 10: Support Services Slide (Slide 5)

    Content:

    • IT Services
    • Library services
    • LMS (Moodle)
    • Research office

    STEP 11: Add Speaker Notes (Slide 5)

    Explain how new staff can access IT, library, and research support.

    STEP 12: Important Policies Slide (Slide 6)

    Content:

    • Attendance policy
    • Examination regulations
    • Leave procedures
    • Code of conduct

    STEP 13: Add Slide Transitions (IMPORTANT)

    Apply Transition to Slides:

    1. Select a slide

    2. Go to View Slide Transition

    3. Choose:

    • Transition: Fade smoothly
    • Duration: Medium
    • Advance slide: On mouse click
    • Click Apply to All Slides

    Smooth, non-distracting transition

    STEP 14: Academic Calendar Slide (Slide 7)

    Content:

    • Semester start date
    • Examination period
    • Vacation periods

    STEP 15: Add Speaker Notes (Slide 7)

    Highlight key academic deadlines and emphasize punctuality.

    STEP 16: Contact Information Slide (Slide 8)

    Content:

    • Deans Office
    • Faculty Office
    • IT Helpdesk
    • Email & phone numbers

    STEP 17: Questions Slide (Slide 9)

    Text:

    Questions & Discussion

    Large font Center aligned

    STEP 18: Thank You Slide (Slide 10)

    Text:

    Thank You

    Optional:

    We wish you success at the Faculty of Science

    STEP 19: Slide Show Preview

    1. Slide Show Start from First Slide

    1. Test:

    2. Animations

    3. Transitions
    4. Speaker notes (Presenter View)

    STEP 20: Save & Export

    Save:

    Orientation_Program_Presentation_2026.odp

    Export as PDF:

    File Export As Export as PDF
